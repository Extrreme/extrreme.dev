About Me

I am a 3rd year Aerospace Engineering student, specializing in Space Systems Design, within the faculty of Engineering and Design at Carleton University in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. In my free time I love to program, which is why stuff like this website exists!

To date, I've used mostly Java for any programming project I've attempted, however I'm hoping to expand this by learning to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in the development of this website, and languages such as C++ for further application development. While knowledge of programming isn't directly related to my field of study in Aerospace Engineering, I believe programming knowledge is never harmful to have. Furthermore, we are moving into a world where everything is digital and online I believe it will become essential for any job to have a basic background in programming. Additionally, there are some programming related subfields of Space-Systems Design such as spacecraft guidance, navigation, and control (GNC) where machine vision and learning is used to enable spacecraft to autonomously avoid obstacles and collisions while traversing space which I find quite interesting.
